## 🧠 Concepts Learned

### 🔹 What is Networking?
Networking is the process of connecting devices to share data and resources.

### 🔹 Types of Networks
- LAN (Local Area Network)
- WAN (Wide Area Network)
- MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

### 🔹 Networking Components
- Router
- Switch
- Modem
- IP Address
- MAC Address

### 🔹 Basic Protocols
- TCP / UDP
- HTTP / HTTPS
- IP

## 🛡️ Cybersecurity Relevance
- Foundation of cybersecurity
- Helps understand attacks & defenses
- Essential for penetration testing
